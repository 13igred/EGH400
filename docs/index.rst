========================================================

.. toctree::

This is documentation for my EGH400 thesis project.

The project goals were to:

* Detect SLSQ (Surf Life Saving Queensland) flags at a beach in a video stream.
* Compile a panoramic view of the beach from a video stream.
* capture the number of people on the beach.
